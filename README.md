# Derlix

> **Proof-of-Verified-Rollout. Real Intelligence. Verified On-Chain.**

Derlix is a decentralized AI infrastructure protocol built on Solana that generates, verifies, and stores Reinforcement Learning (RL) rollouts with cryptographic proof. Every learning trajectory is independently re-executed, verified, and anchored on-chain, creating a transparent and trustless foundation for frontier AI agents.

Unlike traditional AI training pipelines where datasets and rewards cannot easily be verified, Derlix introduces **Proof-of-Verified-Rollout (PoVR)**—a protocol where every rollout can be reproduced, validated, and permanently recorded on Solana. Public project materials describe an architecture where rollouts are independently re-executed, verified, batched into Merkle trees, and anchored on-chain before settlement. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

# ⚙️ How It Works

### Step 1

An AI agent performs an RL task.

↓

### Step 2

A rollout is generated.

↓

### Step 3

The verifier independently replays the rollout.

↓

### Step 4

Rewards are recomputed.

↓

### Step 5

Verified rollouts are batched into a Merkle tree.

↓

### Step 6

The proof is anchored on Solana.

↓

### Step 7

Verified datasets become available for AI training.
